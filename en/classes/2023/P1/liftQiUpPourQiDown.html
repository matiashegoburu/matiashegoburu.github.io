First Level Practitioner Class of Zhi Neng Qigong

This class is designed for those who want to start their journey in the world of qigong in a practical way. We will cover the basic concepts of Qigong practice and focus on learning the first two methods of Zhi Neng Qigong: Lift Qi Up Pour Qi Down, and Three Centers Merge.

These two methods help us charge our body with Qi, and they are the foundation for the success of more complex methods. If there is no Qi in the body, the natural healing mechanisms do not function properly, and the body becomes sick. By recharging our internal battery, the body can reestablish and raise its internal state of balance, step by step.

These two methods are excellent for both healthy people who want to improve their level, and for sick people who are seeking to regain their health. They are extremely safe and have no contraindications.
